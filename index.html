Data Analysis Project
This is a project on Data Analysis. The project is divided into 9 tasks:

1. Draw a chart between Preference and work type where work type=’Intern’. Company size should be below 50000.
2. Draw a chart between company size and company name where company size < 50000.
3. Draw a chart for top 10 companies with max Data analyst as Role and Data scientist as Job title.
4. Draw a chart for Bulgaria and Finland with qualifications B.Tech, M.Tech, or PhD. Work type=’Full time’.
5. Draw a chart for top 5 roles in 2023 and work type=’Intern’.
6. Draw a chart for top 5 companies with Data analyst as Role and Data scientist as Job title. The country name should have 5-7 characters and salary > $100k.
7. Draw a chart for India and Germany with B.Tech qualification, work type=’Full time’, experience > 2 years, job = Data Science, salary > $100k.
8. Draw a chart for top 5 roles in 2023 and work type=’Intern’ where job title should be Account Director, and company size < 2M.
9. Draw a chart for top 20 companies with max User Experience Designer as Role and UI/UX Designer as Job title. The company name should have more than 5 characters.
